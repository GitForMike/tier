Solo Leveling: Arise

게임 소개

Solo Leveling: Arise is an action RPG mobile game based on the popular webtoon and novel series, Solo Leveling. The game follows the story of Sung Jinwoo, a weak hunter who gains the ability to level up infinitely, allowing him to become one of the most powerful hunters in the world. Players can experience the webtoon's original story and also explore new stories created for the game. The game features high-quality 3D graphics and exciting action gameplay.
