Magia Exedra

게임 소개

"Magia Exedra" is a mobile role-playing game based on the popular anime "Puella Magi Madoka Magica." Players become magical girls, undertaking various missions, battling enemies, and progressing through the story. The game offers a new narrative and gameplay system based on the original world and characters, and through collection and nurturing elements, players can build their own powerful magical girl team.
